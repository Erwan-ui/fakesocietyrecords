RWN

Paris-based Erwan Goudard has started working under the RWN alias just a couple of years ago and since then he got to show the world his incredible skills as producers that rapidly let him sign "Underwater" EP on Fake Society, with the Italian heavyweight Christopher Ledger on the remix duties. Having received the support of giants such as Crihan, Cosmjn, Vlad Arapasu, Herodot and Princhindel amongst others, the future definitely shines bright for this young talent.
